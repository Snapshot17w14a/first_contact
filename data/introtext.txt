On the night of Halloween, year 1885, Johnson Whitlock an old tycoon living alone in his mansion went mad and murdered the servants of the house: the plumber, the cleaner, the cook and his loyal butler, after which he ended his own life in a tragic murder-suicide. 100 years later, on the very same night of the massacre, the soul of the butler gets reawakened by an ancient curse. This is your chance to finally free your soul by leaving the estate your body rotted in.
